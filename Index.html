BeFit Cymru

BeFit Cymru recognises that its challenging for you to find the time to try new activities.

Being Active can help you:

• Make new friends

• Spend time with old friends

• Give you a break from your studies
Benefits of being active:

• Improve your sleep pattern and sleep better

• Help you maintain a healthy weight

• Improve concetration and memory skills

• Increase self-confidence

• Learn new skills
