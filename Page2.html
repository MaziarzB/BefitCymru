Tips

Top tips to keep you surf safe:

• Only surf on beaches with lifeguards

• Always tell a responsible adult you are going surfing

• Always surf with a friend

• Always surf with the safety strap around your leg to keep the board close

• Always check the local forecast for wind, swell and tide before leaving home

• Know your limits and be safe – be aware of rip currents

• Choose the correct type of wetsuit

• Be considerate for other surfers and water users.
